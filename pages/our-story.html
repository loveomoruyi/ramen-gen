Our Story

Founded by Brian Chen, Ramen Jin brings the authentic taste of Japanese ramen to Wilcrest Street, Houston. Our unique split venue shares space with My Boss Banh Mi, offering customers a diverse culinary experience.

Brian Chen's passion for authentic Japanese cuisine led him to create Ramen Jin, a restaurant that honors traditional ramen-making techniques while embracing the vibrant food culture of Houston, Texas.

Located on Wilcrest Street, our unique split venue concept pairs Ramen Jin with My Boss Banh Mi, giving customers the best of both Japanese and Vietnamese culinary traditions under one roof. On the left side, you'll find Ramen Jin serving rich, flavorful bowls of ramen made from scratch daily. On the right, My Boss Banh Mi offers fresh, authentic Vietnamese sandwiches and more.

Every bowl at Ramen Jin starts with a carefully prepared broth, simmered for hours to achieve the perfect depth of flavor. We source the freshest ingredients locally and stay true to the recipes that have made Japanese ramen beloved worldwide.

Whether you're a ramen enthusiast or trying it for the first time, we invite you to experience the warmth, flavor, and hospitality that define Ramen Jin. Come visit us and taste the difference that passion makes.
